Title: Research on Control Method for Real-time Hybrid Simulation Based on Reinforcement Learning

A novel Reinforcement learning control method combining a deep deterministic policy gradient algorithm with feedforward compensation is proposed, tested on the underwater shaking table control system and RTHS benchmark problem.

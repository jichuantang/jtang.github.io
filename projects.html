Using DeepONets to Create Spatio-temporal Surrogates for Dynamical Systems
  • Proposed Spatio-temporal full-field DeepONet yields multiple dynamical outputs in single pass.
  • Maximized surrogate expressiveness by enhancing branch-trunk interactions and addressing dynamical inter-dependencies by inherently encoding spatial correlations.
  • Employed three DeepONet architectures to predict cable-stayed bridge model dynamics, demonstrating that Full-field DeepONet provides most accurate predictions with greatest efficiency.
Leveraging Multi-Fidelity Models for Computational Shock Response Prediction
  • Performed model analysis of ship structures to impulsive loading inputs, and discerned the full discrepancies between the low- and high-fidelity models.
  • Conducted a comprehensive parametric study of proposed approaches of Deep Operator Networks (DeepONets) and Recurrent Neural Networks (e.g., RNN, LSTM and GRU) for residual modeling of the dynamical system.
  • Implemented multi-fidelity surrogates by leveraging strategies of transfer leanring and residual learning.
Urban Smart Transportation and Safety Maintenance
  • Conducted denoising calibration and performance evaluation of MEMS accelerometers in low-frequency noise impact and validated its adaptability to different noise sources by using Generative adversarial networks (GANs), with comparison to empirical modal decomposition and wavelet transform methods.
  • Developed a LabVIEW-based host computer for MEMS accelerometer server and performed durability testing (e.g., temperature, salt spray).
  • Debugged hardware links of synchronization systems (e.g., encoders, synchronization boxes, trigger boxes) on tunnel diagnosis vehicles for hidden disease detection.
Human-induced Vibration Control of Footbridge with Reinforcement Learning
  • Constructed the dynamics model for the coupled system of concrete pedestrian bridge, human and semi-active tuned mass damper (STMD) considering human-induced vibration.
  • Introduced the twin delayed deep deterministic policy gradient (TD3) algorithm for RL agent to enhance STMD and reduce vibration under both periodic and random human-induced dynamic loading.
  • Investigated the influence of model hyperparameters (e.g., learning rate, discount factor) on control performance and proposed a general scheme of RL-based control for pedestrian bridge with STMD.
RL-based Control Method for Real-Time Hybrid Simulation
  • Developed digital twin model of underwater shaking table based on Simulink to simulate structural behaviors under wave and earthquake loads with high accuracy, verified by physical experimental data.
  • Trained RL agent as controller with deep deterministic policy gradient (DDPG) algorithm to improve the time- delay and tracking error in the worst condition by 6.54% and 7.52% compared to model-based controller, and by 123.48% and 89.95% compared to no compensation method.
  • Extended DDPG with feedforward (FF) compensation as a novel RL method to more accurately and robustly control the transfer system with model uncertainty in RTHS and reached only 3.28% maximum pertuation while PI and FF controller reached 5.88% and 10.57%.
